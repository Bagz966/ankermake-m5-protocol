Connecting PrusaSlicer/SuperSlicer

  1. Go to "Printer settings" tab
  2. Click "Gear"⚙️ (Add/Edit Physical Printer)
  3. Fill in "Descriptive name for the printer" with whatever you like
  4. Select your Ankermake printer instructions from the dropdown menu
  5. Under "Host Type" select "OctoPrint"
  6. In "Hostname, IP or URL" fill in with: {{ request_host }}:{{ request_port }}
  7. Leave "API Key / Password" blank
  8. Click "Test" to validate input
  9. Click "Ok" to close the window
  10. Slice your file as normal
  11. Click "G>" button to start direct upload to the printer
  12. Click "Upload and print" - the "Upload"-only is not supported
  13. Enjoy printing with ankerctl 😉

Hint: Ctrl+Shift+G will slice and open the upload to printer window
